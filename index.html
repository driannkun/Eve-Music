    EVE

    Singer - Songwriter - Vocaloid Producer

    After 2 self-produced msuic albums, Eve released the national distribution board album「 OFFICIAL NUMBER 」. The indie album「Culture」released on December 13, 2017 was his first all-self-composed songs,「Dramaturgy」「As You Like It」 which was included in this album, has exceeded 100 million plays. The number of YouTube subscribers exceeds 4 million.

    The one-man live shows in 2018, 2019 including the 「winter tour 2019-2020 turbulent dining table」 mobilized 12,000 people, The tickets were sold out immediately. On February 6, 2019, the Album 「Otogi」 was released, 「Smile」released on February 12, 2020 won the 2nd place on the Oricon album chart, and 「Kaikaikitan / Aono Waltz」 released on December 23, 2020 won the 1st place, and the recorded song “Kaikai Kitan” exceeded 300 million the streaming, and YouTube exceeded 250 million playbacks.
